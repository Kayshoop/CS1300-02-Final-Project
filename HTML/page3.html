Why weather apps are important!

Knowing the weather is crucial for ensuring safety, as it helps people prepare for severe conditions like storms, floods, or extreme temperatures. It supports effective planning in various industries, such as agriculture, construction, and transportation, minimizing disruptions and losses. Weather forecasts enable individuals to plan outdoor activities and travel, reducing risks associated with adverse conditions. It also plays a vital role in environmental protection, as timely warnings about extreme weather events can help mitigate damage to ecosystems. Additionally, understanding the weather supports public health by guiding measures to protect against heatwaves, cold spells, or air pollution.
